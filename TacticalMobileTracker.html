Summary

An accurate mobile phone direction finder with active capabilities and supports 2G, 3G and 4G. It consists of the Vehicle mounted or portable, and Direction finder

Vehicle mounted Device consists of multi BTS, PA, Antenna, Power, etc. It can set up multi BTS at the same time and force all nearby mobile phones and other cellular data devices to connect to it. Once the target device connected, it will continuously request the target device’s location information. Other devices will be denied to access without affecting its normal communication. Great mobility and adaptability make it suit for targets separated in different places.

Direction finder is responsible to locate the target accurately by detecting field strength. It’s easy to be carried and controlled by an Android system via Bluetooth connection, which makes it covertly.

Could be useful in various scenarios such as antidrug operations, counter-terrorist activities and criminal investigations. LEA can find the precise position of the criminal with the tracker efficiently.

Features

Environment Survey Function

Automatically scan the peripheral public network frequency points and give suggestions for configuration.

IMSI Catcher Function

Get surrounding mobile phones’ IMSI and IMEI.

Blacklist Function

Support adding multiple targets to blacklist.

Positioning Function

Support starting or stopping positioning manually or switching to position another target without re-setup the cell.

Frequency Redirect

Our device can redirect the target to the specified 2, 3, 4G or other specified frequency points.

GPS Trace

The trace of one target could be shown on a map.

All Bands Supported and Customizable

detect and locate the handset supporting 2G/3G/4G network. And all bands are customable according to customer requirements

Long Range

Vehicle Device working range can be up to 1200m. Portable Device working range reaches to 700m.

High Accuracy

Vehicle Device’s pinpoint accuracy can be up to 5 meters. Portable Device’s pinpoint accuracy can be up to 1 meter.
